Bits + Blocks Lab is a startup creation lab.

We understand what's on the horizon by building things today. The technological landscape is morphing faster than ever. Bits + Blocks Lab from IDEO is startup creation lab whose mission is to understand the future of trust, transactions, and reputation by building ventures today. Using startups as our means to understand the emerging technologies of Bitcoin and block chains, Bits + Blocks Lab creates new products and services that will profoundly reshape existing markets and create new ones.

We're creating the optimal conditions for startups to emerge.

We’ve assembled 24 of Boston’s most talented and inspiring students. Together with the Harvard iLab, we’ve created a program that explores the possibilities enabled by the block chain. Collaborating with the student entrepreneurs will be IDEO designers, industry experts, lead users, and experts from the worlds of Bitcoin and the block chain. We’ll spend the summer building six new ventures inspired by the block chain.
